Content

Home page

The Home page consists of an article of customer service languages, why to use them and why not to use some words.

It deals with introducing yourself and outros, asking for surveys, clarifying questions, and empathizing with customers.

This page will have my image as the author and will also have customer servicey images

Canned Responses

This page does not need any images but is an inteeractive page where users can see a list of customer service languages in their category and pick from them. They can also favorite service languages they like.
